Standard repayment splits what a borrower owes, plus interest, into 120 equal payments over 10 years. Your loans will be put in standard repayment unless you request a different payment plan. Its 10-year repayment period is the shortest of any repayment plan—so it creates the highest payments.

Things to know

- If you can’t make your payments on time, you could be assessed late fees, and receive negative marks on your credit history.

- Later in this activity, we will discuss several different options that can help if you need lower payments.

- $50 is the minimum monthly payment for standard repayment.
