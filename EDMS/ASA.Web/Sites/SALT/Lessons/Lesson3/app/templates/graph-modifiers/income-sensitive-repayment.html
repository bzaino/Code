Things to know

-ISR will only lower your monthly payments for 5 years. After that, you’ll need to get a new repayment plan.

-Income-sensitive repayment is only offered for loans in the Federal Family Education Loan Program, which stopped issuing loans in 2010. If you have borrowed any loans after that time, you should look into Income-based repayment.
